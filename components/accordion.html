Common side effects may include stomach upset, heartburn, drowsiness, or mild headache. More serious side effects can include stomach bleeding, allergic reactions, or ringing in the ears. Contact your doctor if you experience any severe or persistent side effects.

For adults and children 12 years and over: Take 1-2 tablets every 4 hours as needed. Do not exceed 12 tablets in 24 hours unless directed by a doctor. Take with food or milk if stomach upset occurs. Do not give to children under 12 years without consulting a doctor.

Do not use if you are allergic to aspirin or any other pain reliever/fever reducer. Ask a doctor before use if you have stomach problems, high blood pressure, heart disease, liver cirrhosis, kidney disease, asthma, or bleeding problems. Stop use and ask a doctor if symptoms persist for more than 10 days.

Features

  • Smooth animations: CSS transitions for expand/collapse

  • Focus management: Visual focus indicators

  • Single-open mode: Use .accordion-group wrapper
  • Responsive: Works across all screen sizes
